Our Approach

Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition

West Company Partners is actively searching to acquire a profitable, well-established small business to own and operate for the long term. Unlike private equity firms focused on quick returns, we're seeking a single business to dedicate ourselves to completely.

Our approach, known as Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition (ETA), combines the energy and commitment of hands-on ownership with respect for what's already working. We believe the best businesses are built on strong foundations laid by their current owners.

We're not looking to flip a business or make dramatic changes. We want to learn from the current owner, understand what makes the business successful, and build upon that foundation with fresh perspective and renewed energy.

Long-Term Commitment

We're not looking for a quick exit. We want to own and operate for decades.

People First

Employees and customers are the heart of any great business.

Sustainable Growth

Building value steadily through operational excellence.

Our Investment Criteria

Business Profile

  • Established business with 10+ years of operating history
  • Strong reputation in its market or community
  • Loyal customer base with recurring revenue characteristics
  • Defensible market position or competitive advantages

Financial Characteristics

  • Annual revenue between $750k and $3M
  • Consistent profitability with healthy margins
  • Clean financial records and stable cash flows

Industries of Interest

  • Business services (B2B)
  • Light manufacturing and distribution
  • Specialty trade contractors
  • Healthcare services
  • Other niche service businesses

Ideal Situation

  • Owner seeking retirement or new chapter
  • Willingness to support transition period
  • Strong management team or key employees in place
